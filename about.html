About Me

the man behind the code


Howdy!

I am currently a Junior at Texas A&M studying computer science with a minor in cyber security. My background in programming stems back to high school where I began my interest in software development. I started learning the basics of programming in my spare time after school, and I was greatly intrigued by the wide variety of applications that software entails. Fast forward to today and that same passion is evermore present. I have a great foundation in software that is constantly being built upon. Being able to create a program and see it in action is truly amazing, and I look forward to innovating the latest and greatest in technology.


Although I enjoy programming, there is much more to me than software development! In my free time, I love to play any pick up sport that I can. Having an active lifestyle helps with both the stress of school and my physical health, so I love being able to get together with friends and play sports. I regularly play both flag football and basketball; I am a naturally competitive person, so I am always looking for opportunities to play a game!


Another major part of who I am is my identity as a Christian. Coming to college has strengthened my religious beliefs immensely; I am currently a member of Fellowship Church, and I attend meetings three times a week. My church fosters a community of passion and kindness, and it is heavily based on outreach to college students. Being able to spread the word of God is incredible, and I get so many great opportunities to do that as a college student. Being an active member of this church has been a huge blessing in my life, and its impacts go beyond what I can put into words.
